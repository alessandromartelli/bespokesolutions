## Wiki

The wiki is Claude-maintained. Every research session, decision, and concept gets captured here and compounds over time.

| Command | What it does |
|---|---|
| `/wiki <topic>` | Research a topic and add it to the wiki |
| `/save` | Save the current conversation as a wiki entry |
| `/ingest <url>` | Feed Claude a URL, it extracts and integrates it |

---

## Inbox

Drop raw notes in [[inbox/]] — Claude processes them into the wiki with `/wiki process-inbox`.
