# Prospect Research — Manufacturing BAB + South of France

---

## Target Profile

**Geography**: Biarritz-Bayonne-Anglet (dept 64) + Occitanie + PACA
**Vertical**: Manufacturing SMEs, 20–250 employees
**Pain**: Heavy manual processes in planning, procurement, inventory, reporting

---

## Data Sources

### Public Company Data
- **Recherche Entreprises API** — `recherche-entreprises.api.gouv.fr` — free, no auth. SIREN, directors, financials, NAF code, employee band. Filter by NAF + department + size.
- **BODACC** — company event registry. Sales, creations, modifications, restructurings.
- **INSEE / Sirene** — national company registry, cross-reference for firmographic data.

### Job Boards
- **HelloWork** — scrapable. SMEs post here, large Tier-1s use own portals — natural SME filter.
- **APEC** — cadre/executive roles only. Signals director-level hires.
- **France Travail API** — structured, filterable by ROME code + department. Free, OAuth2.
- **LinkedIn Jobs** — via Composio.

### Cluster Directories
- **Aerospace Valley** — 176+ manufacturing members, South of France. `aerospace-valley.com/fr/membres`
- **UIMM** — industrial employers federation, regional chapters.
- **French Fab** — national manufacturing network directory.

### Direct Intelligence
- Company websites
- LinkedIn company pages
- Local press (Sud Ouest, La Dépêche)
